About Me

Hi, my name is Solomon and I'm a problem solver. Coming up with innovative solutions to the problems I encounter is what I do. And when a solution creates some real change in the world, that's when things get exciting. I often put myself into challenging situations. I usually get out of them by learning something new. In fact, my attachment to hardship and eagerness to learn is probably what brought me into programming in the first place.

My first exposure to programming was nearly a decade ago. I took a course over the summer of '09 and have been in love with it ever since. I like to think I've learnt a lot since then. Right now, I'm a self taught full stack developer leaning towards all things web developement related.

Check out my work and feel free to shoot me an email if you think we'd work great together.
